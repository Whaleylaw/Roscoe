# Kentucky Trust Account Requirements

## Overview

Kentucky Rules of Professional Conduct Rule 1.15 governs the handling of client funds. Compliance is mandatory for all settlement distributions.

---

## Core Requirements

### KRPC 1.15 - Safekeeping Property

1. **Separate Account:** Client funds must be held in a client trust account (IOLTA)
2. **Prompt Deposit:** Settlement funds deposited promptly upon receipt
3. **No Commingling:** Client and firm funds kept separate
4. **Prompt Delivery:** Funds delivered to client upon request after entitled
5. **Complete Records:** Detailed records maintained

---

## Settlement Check Handling

### Step 1: Receive Check

1. Verify check amount matches settlement
2. Verify payees (may include firm and client)
3. Note receipt date

### Step 2: Deposit

1. Deposit to IOLTA trust account
2. **NOT** operating account
3. Record deposit with case reference

### Step 3: Hold Period

**Wait for check to clear:**
- Certified/cashier's check: 1-2 business days
- Regular check: 5-10 business days
- Large amounts: May require longer hold

**DO NOT disburse until cleared.**

### Step 4: Distribute

Only after cleared:
1. Pay all liens first (if disputed, hold in trust)
2. Transfer attorney fee to operating
3. Transfer costs reimbursement to operating
4. Issue client distribution

---

## Record Keeping Requirements

### Required Records

| Record | Retention |
|--------|-----------|
| Check images | 6 years |
| Deposit slips | 6 years |
| Disbursement records | 6 years |
| Settlement statements | 6 years |
| Client ledgers | 6 years |

### Per-Client Ledger

Track for each client:
- Date of each transaction
- Source of funds
- Purpose of disbursement
- Running balance

---

## IOLTA Account Rules

### Interest on Lawyers Trust Accounts

- Required for client funds
- Interest goes to Kentucky IOLTA Fund
- Not to lawyer or client
- Supports legal aid services

### Account Requirements

- Must be in Kentucky bank
- Must be IOLTA-approved
- Must have "Trust Account" or "IOLTA" designation
- Must notify Kentucky Bar

---

## Disbursement Order

**Required Order:**

1. **Undisputed Liens First**
   - Medicare
   - Medicaid  
   - Hospital liens
   - Provider liens

2. **Then Attorney Disbursements**
   - Attorney fee to operating
   - Cost reimbursement to operating

3. **Finally Client Distribution**
   - Only after liens cleared
   - After check cleared
   - Per settlement statement

---

## Disputed Liens

If lien amount disputed:

1. **Hold Disputed Amount**
   - Keep in trust account
   - Document hold reason
   
2. **Distribute Undisputed**
   - Pay agreed amounts
   - Distribute clear funds to client

3. **Resolve Dispute**
   - May require interpleader
   - May require negotiation
   - Document resolution

4. **Final Distribution**
   - After dispute resolved
   - Per final agreement

---

## Common Violations to Avoid

| Violation | Consequence |
|-----------|-------------|
| Commingling | Disciplinary action |
| Disbursing before clear | Trust account deficit |
| Failing to pay liens | Ethical and legal liability |
| Inadequate records | Audit failure |
| Delay in distribution | Client complaint |
