# Intake Forms Index

## Overview

This folder contains fillable PDF templates for all intake documents used in Phase 0: Onboarding. These are the actual forms sent to clients for completion and signature.

---

## Landmark Documents (Required)

These three documents are mandatory for Phase 0 completion:

| Document | Filename | Case Types |
|----------|----------|------------|
| New Client Information Sheet | `2021 Whaley New Client Information Sheet (1).pdf` | All |
| MVA Fee Agreement | `2021 Whaley MVA Fee Agreement (1).pdf` | MVA |
| S&F Fee Agreement | `2021 Whaley S&F Fee Agreement (1).pdf` | S&F |
| WC Fee Agreement | `2021 Whaley WC Fee Agreement - Final (1).pdf` | WC |
| Medical Authorization (HIPAA) | `2021 Whaley Medical Authorization (HIPAA) (1).pdf` | All |

---

## Additional Documents (All Cases)

| Document | Filename | Purpose |
|----------|----------|---------|
| Medical Treatment Questionnaire | `2021 Whaley Medical Treatment Questionnaire (1).pdf` | Details about injuries and treatment history |
| Digital Signature Authorization | `2021 Whaley Authorization of Digitally Signature Replication (1).pdf` | Authorizes electronic signature use |

## Document Selection by Case Type

### MVA (Motor Vehicle Accident)

**Required:**
- `2021 Whaley New Client Information Sheet (1).pdf`
- `2021 Whaley MVA Fee Agreement (1).pdf`
- `2021 Whaley Medical Authorization (HIPAA) (1).pdf`

**Additional:**
- `2021 Whaley Medical Treatment Questionnaire (1).pdf`
- `2021 Whaley Authorization of Digitally Signature Replication (1).pdf`
- `2021 Whaley MVA Accident Detail Information Sheet (1).pdf`

**Conditional:**
- `2021 Whaley Wage & Salary Verification (1).pdf` (if employed)
- `2021 Whaley CMS Medicare Verification Form (1).pdf` (if Medicare)

### S&F (Slip and Fall)

**Required:**
- `2021 Whaley New Client Information Sheet (1).pdf`
- `2021 Whaley S&F Fee Agreement (1).pdf`
- `2021 Whaley Medical Authorization (HIPAA) (1).pdf`

**Additional:**
- `2021 Whaley Medical Treatment Questionnaire (1).pdf`
- `2021 Whaley Authorization of Digitally Signature Replication (1).pdf`
- `2021 Whaley S&F Accident Detail Information Sheet (1).pdf`

**Conditional:**
- `2021 Whaley Wage & Salary Verification (1).pdf` (if employed)
- `2021 Whaley CMS Medicare Verification Form (1).pdf` (if Medicare)

### WC (Workers' Compensation)

**Required:**
- `2021 Whaley New Client Information Sheet (1).pdf`
- `2021 Whaley WC Fee Agreement - Final (1).pdf`
- `2021 Whaley Medical Authorization (HIPAA) (1).pdf`

**Additional:**
- `2021 Whaley Medical Treatment Questionnaire (1).pdf`
- `2021 Whaley Authorization of Digitally Signature Replication (1).pdf`
- `2021 Whaley Wage & Salary Verification (1).pdf` (always required for WC)

**Conditional:**
- `2021 Whaley CMS Medicare Verification Form (1).pdf` (if Medicare)

---

## Usage Notes

1. **All PDFs are fillable** - They can be completed electronically before signing
2. **Fee Agreement varies by case type** - Always use the correct version
3. **WC always requires wage verification** - Employment is inherent to the claim
4. **Medicare form is age-dependent** - Required if client is 65+ or approaching 65
