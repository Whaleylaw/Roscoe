# Rebuttal Expert Timing Reference

## When Rebuttal Expert Needed

Consider rebuttal expert when defense:
- Has IME challenging injuries
- Has accident reconstructionist
- Has economic expert attacking damages
- Has liability expert

---

## Deadline Strategy

### Standard Timeline

```
Day 0:   Discovery cutoff
Day -90: Our expert disclosure
Day -60: Their expert disclosure
Day -30: Rebuttal expert disclosure
Day -14: Expert discovery cutoff
Day 0:   Trial
```

### Strategic Considerations

**Wait Before Retaining Rebuttal:**
- See what their expert actually says
- May not need rebuttal if their expert weak
- Save expense if possible

**Early Rebuttal Retention:**
- Complex issues requiring preparation
- Expert has limited availability
- Need expert deposition before cutoff

---

## Rebuttal Expert Scope

Rebuttal experts can only:
- Rebut opinions of other side's experts
- Cannot introduce new subject matter
- Must be truly responsive

**Improper Rebuttal:**
```
❌ "Our rebuttal economist will calculate lost wages"
   (if we didn't disclose economist originally)
```

**Proper Rebuttal:**
```
✓ "Our rebuttal economist will respond to defense 
   economist's methodology criticism"
```

---

## Common Rebuttal Scenarios

### Defense IME

**Their position:** Plaintiff not injured / fully recovered / pre-existing

**Rebuttal options:**
- Treating physician testimony on causation
- Independent examination by our expert
- Medical literature response

### Defense Accident Reconstruction

**Their position:** Plaintiff at fault / low impact

**Rebuttal options:**
- Our reconstructionist
- Biomechanical engineer
- Challenge methodology

### Defense Economist

**Their position:** No future damages / inflated claims

**Rebuttal options:**
- Our economist with different methodology
- Vocational expert on employability
- Life care planner on future medical

---

## Disclosure Content for Rebuttal

Must specify:
- What opinion being rebutted
- Expert's contrary opinion
- Basis for disagreement

```
REBUTTAL EXPERT DISCLOSURE

In rebuttal to Defendant's expert [Name], who opined that 
[opinion], Plaintiff discloses [Rebuttal Expert Name] who 
will testify that [contrary opinion] because [basis].
```
