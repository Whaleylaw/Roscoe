# Sub-Phase 7.5: Trial - Landmarks

## Landmark Summary

| ID | Landmark | Type | Workflow |
|----|----------|------|----------|
| 7.5.1 | Jury Selected | Progress | conduct_trial |
| 7.5.2 | Plaintiff's Case Presented | Progress | conduct_trial |

| 7.5.4 | Verdict Rendered | **Hard Blocker** | conduct_trial |

---

## Landmark Definitions

### 7.5.1: Jury Selected

**Verification:** Jury empaneled and sworn.

### 7.5.2: Plaintiff's Case Presented

**Verification:** Plaintiff rested case-in-chief.

### 7.5.3: All Evidence Closed

**Verification:** Both sides rested, evidence closed.

### 7.5.4: Verdict Rendered ⭐

**Hard Blocker**

**Verification:** Jury returned verdict OR directed verdict entered OR other resolution.

---

## Exit to Phase 5 or 8

**Required:** 7.5.4 (Verdict Rendered)

- If verdict for plaintiff → Phase 5 (Settlement) for collection
- If verdict for defendant → Phase 8 (Closed) or appeal
