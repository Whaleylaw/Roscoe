### COUNT II: VICARIOUS LIABILITY - [defendant.employer.name]

24. [defendant.employer.name] owed a duty to the public, including Plaintiff, to exercise reasonable care in hiring, retaining, and supervising its employees who operate motor vehicles.

25. [defendant.employee.name]'s driving record and/or other characteristics were such that a reasonably prudent employer would have, or should have, known that by allowing [defendant.employee.name] to operate vehicles, third persons would be in danger.

26. [defendant.employer.name] hired and/or retained [defendant.employee.name] without properly assessing qualifications and/or without properly conducting a background check.
