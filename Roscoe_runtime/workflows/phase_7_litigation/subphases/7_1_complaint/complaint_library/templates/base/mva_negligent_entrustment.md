# MVA Complaint with Negligent Entrustment

## AGENT INSTRUCTIONS

This template is for an MVA complaint where the vehicle owner entrusted the vehicle to an unfit driver. Use when:
- Vehicle owner is different from driver
- Owner knew or should have known driver was unfit
- Driver had history of reckless driving, DUI, etc.
- No employment relationship (use vicarious liability template if employed)

**Legal Basis:**
- *McGrew v. Stone*, 998 S.W.2d 5 (Ky. 1999)
- *Brady v. B&B Ice Co.*, 242 Ky. 138, 45 S.W.2d 1051 (Ky. 1931)

**Key Element:** Owner knew or should have known driver was inexperienced, careless, or reckless.
