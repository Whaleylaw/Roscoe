# Premises Liability Complaint - Dog Bite / Animal Attack

## AGENT INSTRUCTIONS

This template is for a premises liability complaint involving a dog bite or animal attack. Use when:
- Injury caused by animal owned/harbored by defendant
- Occurred on or near defendant's property
- Dog bite or other animal attack

**Legal Basis:**
- Negligence (most common)
- Strict liability may apply if owner knew of vicious propensities

**Key Elements:**
1. Defendant owned/harbored the animal
2. Animal caused injury to plaintiff
3. Defendant knew or should have known of animal's dangerous propensities
4. Defendant failed to control the animal
