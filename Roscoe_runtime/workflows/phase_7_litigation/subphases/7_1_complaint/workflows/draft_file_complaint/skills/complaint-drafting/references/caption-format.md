# Caption Format Reference

## Kentucky Circuit Court Caption

```
COMMONWEALTH OF KENTUCKY
[COUNTY NAME] CIRCUIT COURT
DIVISION [NUMBER]

CIVIL ACTION NO. ____________

[PLAINTIFF NAME],
                    PLAINTIFF,

v.

[DEFENDANT NAME],
                    DEFENDANT.

* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *

                COMPLAINT

* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *
```

---

## Party Naming Conventions

### Individual Plaintiffs
```
JOHN DOE,
                    PLAINTIFF,
```

### Multiple Plaintiffs
```
JOHN DOE and JANE DOE,
                    PLAINTIFFS,
```

### Individual Defendants
```
ABC COMPANY, INC.,
                    DEFENDANT.
```

### Multiple Defendants
```
ABC COMPANY, INC. and
JOHN SMITH,
                    DEFENDANTS.
```

---

## Corporate Defendants

### Kentucky Corporation
```
ABC COMPANY, INC., a Kentucky corporation,
```

### Foreign Corporation
```
XYZ CORPORATION, a Delaware corporation
authorized to do business in Kentucky,
```

### Unknown Corporate Status
```
ABC COMPANY, a business entity of unknown form,
```

---

## Doe Defendants

When defendant identity unknown:
```
JOHN DOE, identity unknown, operator of
vehicle bearing Kentucky license plate XXX-XXX,
```

---

## Case Style Examples

### Single Plaintiff v. Single Defendant
```
JOHN DOE v. ABC COMPANY, INC.
```

### Multiple Parties
```
JOHN DOE, et al. v. ABC COMPANY, INC., et al.
```

---

## Font and Formatting

- Font: Times New Roman, 12pt (preferred)
- Margins: 1 inch all sides
- Line spacing: Double-spaced for body
- Caption: Single-spaced
- Page numbers: Bottom center
