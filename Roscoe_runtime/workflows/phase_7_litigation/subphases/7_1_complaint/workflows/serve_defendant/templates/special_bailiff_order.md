## ORDER APPOINTING SPECIAL BAILIFF

---

This matter having come before the Court on Plaintiff's Motion for Appointment of Special Bailiff, and the Court having reviewed the Motion and accompanying Affidavit of Good Cause, and being otherwise sufficiently advised:

**IT IS HEREBY ORDERED** that **[special_bailiff.name]** is appointed as Special Bailiff for the limited purpose of serving process upon Defendant, **[defendant.name]**, in the above-captioned action.

**IT IS FURTHER ORDERED** that said Special Bailiff shall have the authority to serve the Summons and Complaint upon Defendant at any location within the Commonwealth of Kentucky.

**IT IS FURTHER ORDERED** that upon service, the Special Bailiff shall file a return of service with the Clerk of this Court, setting forth:

1. The date and time of service;
2. The location where service was made;
3. The manner of service;
4. A description of the person served.

**IT IS FURTHER ORDERED** that the Plaintiff shall be responsible for payment of the Special Bailiff's fees and expenses.
