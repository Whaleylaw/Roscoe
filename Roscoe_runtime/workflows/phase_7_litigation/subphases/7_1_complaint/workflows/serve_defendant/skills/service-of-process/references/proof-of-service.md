# Proof of Service Reference

## Required Documentation by Method

### Personal Service - Sheriff Return

Sheriff files:
- Date of service
- Time of service
- Location of service
- Description of person served
- Manner of service
- Sheriff's signature

**Filed by:** Sheriff (automatic)
**Your action:** Verify filed and accurate

---

### Personal Service - Private Process Server

Server files affidavit stating:
- Server is over 18 and not a party
- Date, time, location of service
- Description of person served
- Manner of delivery
- Server's signature (notarized)

**Template:**
```
AFFIDAVIT OF SERVICE

STATE OF KENTUCKY
COUNTY OF [COUNTY]

    I, [SERVER NAME], being duly sworn, state:

    1. I am over 18 years of age and not a party to this action.

    2. On [DATE], at approximately [TIME], I served [DEFENDANT NAME]
       with the Summons and Complaint in this action.

    3. Service was made at [ADDRESS].

    4. I served the documents by [delivering them personally to the
       defendant / leaving them at the defendant's feet after they
       refused to accept them].

    5. I identified the person served as [DEFENDANT NAME] by
       [method of identification].

                    _________________________
                    [Server Name]

    Subscribed and sworn before me this ___ day of _______, 20__.

                    _________________________
                    Notary Public
    My commission expires: _______________
```

---

### Certified Mail - Return Receipt

File with court:
- Completed return receipt (green card)
- Affidavit of mailing

**Green Card Must Show:**
- Defendant's signature
- Date of delivery
- Restricted delivery checked

**Affidavit of Mailing:**
```
AFFIDAVIT OF SERVICE BY CERTIFIED MAIL

    I, [NAME], state that on [DATE], I caused to be mailed by
    United States Certified Mail, Return Receipt Requested,
    Restricted Delivery, a copy of the Summons and Complaint
    in this action, addressed to:

        [DEFENDANT NAME]
        [ADDRESS]

    Attached hereto is a true copy of the return receipt showing
    delivery on [DATE].

                    _________________________
                    [Name]
```

---

### Secretary of State Service

Documentation required:
1. Affidavit showing basis for long-arm jurisdiction
2. Proof of service on Secretary of State
3. Secretary of State's certificate of mailing to defendant
4. Return receipt (if returned)

---

### Warning Order

Documentation required:
1. Motion for warning order
2. Affidavit of diligent search
3. Court's warning order
4. Proof of publication
5. Affidavit of publication from newspaper
