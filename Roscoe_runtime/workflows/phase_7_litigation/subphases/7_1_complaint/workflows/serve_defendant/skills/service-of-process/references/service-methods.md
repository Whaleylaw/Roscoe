# Service Methods Reference

## Kentucky Service of Process Rules (CR 4)

### Personal Service (CR 4.04)

**Most Reliable Method**

Service by:
- Sheriff of county where defendant found
- Constable
- Appointed process server
- Any person over 18 not a party

**Execution:**
- Deliver copy of summons and complaint to defendant personally
- If defendant refuses, leave at their feet

**Documentation:**
- Return of service filed by server
- States date, time, location, manner of service

---

### Certified Mail (CR 4.01)

**Alternative to Personal Service**

Requirements:
- Return receipt requested
- Restricted delivery (defendant must sign)
- Green card returned with signature

**Execution:**
1. Mail summons and complaint via USPS certified mail
2. Return receipt must be signed by defendant
3. If signed by someone else, service may be challenged

**Documentation:**
- Green card filed with court
- Affidavit of mailing

**Limitations:**
- Defendant can refuse to sign
- May need to follow up with personal service

---

### Service on Corporation (CR 4.04(4))

**Kentucky Corporation:**
- Registered agent
- Officer
- Managing agent
- Any agent authorized to receive service

**Foreign Corporation (authorized in KY):**
- Registered agent
- Officer or managing agent in Kentucky

**How to Find Registered Agent:**
- Kentucky Secretary of State website
- Business entity search

---

### Secretary of State Service (CR 4.04(7))

**For Out-of-State Defendants**

When available:
- Non-resident motorist (long-arm statute)
- Non-resident doing business in Kentucky
- Non-resident causing injury in Kentucky

**Process:**
1. Serve Secretary of State as statutory agent
2. SOS forwards to defendant by certified mail
3. Return receipt or affidavit of mailing filed

**Timeline:**
- Allow extra time for SOS forwarding
- 30+ days typical

---

### Warning Order (CR 4.05)

**When Defendant Cannot Be Located**

Requirements:
- Good faith effort to locate defendant
- Affidavit detailing search efforts
- Court approval

**Process:**
1. File motion for warning order
2. Submit affidavit of search efforts
3. Court issues warning order
4. Publish in newspaper (per court order)
5. File proof of publication

**Search Efforts to Document:**
- Last known addresses
- DMV records
- Voter registration
- Social media
- Skip tracing
- Relatives contacted

---

### Service on Partnerships

- Serve any partner
- Serve managing agent
- Serve agent for service of process

---

### Service on Government Entities

**Kentucky State:**
- Attorney General

**Kentucky County/City:**
- County Attorney or City Attorney
- Chief executive officer

**Federal:**
- US Attorney for district
- Plus Attorney General in DC

---

## Service Deadlines

| Event | Deadline |
|-------|----------|
| Service after filing | 90 days (CR 4.01(2)) |
| Extension request | Before 90 days expires |
| Consequence of failure | Dismissal without prejudice |

## Special Bailiff (Jefferson County)

**When Personal Service Fails Repeatedly**

A Special Bailiff is a private process server appointed by the court when the Sheriff or regular service methods have failed.

### When to Use

- Multiple failed service attempts by Sheriff
- Defendant actively evading service
- Time-sensitive cases requiring faster service
- Defendant at locations difficult for Sheriff to access

### Requirements (Jefferson County)

1. **Affidavit of Good Cause** showing:
   - Prior service attempts and failures
   - Why regular service is inadequate
   - Need for Special Bailiff appointment

2. **Proposed Order** appointing Special Bailiff

3. **Use Approved Process Server**:
   - Lou Metro Process Services
   - Other court-approved servers (check with Clerk)

### Process

1. File Motion requesting appointment of Special Bailiff
2. Attach Affidavit of Good Cause
3. Attach Proposed Order
4. Submit to Judge for signature
5. Once signed, contact appointed Special Bailiff
6. Provide copies of Summons and Complaint
7. Special Bailiff serves defendant
8. Special Bailiff files return of service

### Fees

- Court filing fee for motion
- Special Bailiff service fee (typically $50-150 per defendant)
- May vary by process server

### Templates

- `special_bailiff_affidavit.md` - Affidavit of Good Cause
- `special_bailiff_order.md` - Order Appointing Special Bailiff

## Problem Resolution

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Defendant avoids server | Try different times/locations, then Special Bailiff |
| Defendant refuses | "Substituted service" at feet |
| Cannot locate | Warning order |
| Wrong address | Skip trace, try new address |
| Corporate agent resigned | Serve SOS or officer |
| Multiple failed attempts | Special Bailiff (Jefferson Co.) |
