# Interrogatory Templates Reference

## Standard PI Interrogatory Topics

### Identity and Background

```
1. State your full legal name, date of birth, current address, and 
   all addresses at which you have resided during the past five years.

2. State your Social Security number and driver's license number, 
   including the issuing state.

3. Identify all persons who have knowledge of any facts relevant 
   to this lawsuit, stating for each: name, address, telephone 
   number, and the subject matter of their knowledge.
```

### Accident Facts

```
4. Describe in detail your version of how the accident occurred, 
   including your location, direction of travel, speed, and all 
   actions you took immediately before, during, and after the 
   collision.

5. Identify all documents, photographs, or other tangible things 
   that relate to or depict the accident scene, the vehicles 
   involved, or the injuries claimed.

6. State whether you made any statements, written or oral, to 
   anyone regarding the accident, and if so, identify to whom, 
   when, and the substance of each statement.
```

### Insurance Information

```
7. Identify all insurance policies that may provide coverage for 
   the claims made in this lawsuit, including: company name, 
   policy number, policy limits, and the name of the insured.

8. State whether you have liability insurance coverage, and if 
   so, state the policy limits for bodily injury per person 
   and per occurrence.
```

### Witness Information

```
9. Identify all persons who witnessed any part of the accident, 
   stating for each: name, address, telephone number, and what 
   they witnessed.

10. Identify all persons whom you expect to call as witnesses 
    at trial, and for each state: name, address, area of 
    expected testimony.
```

### Expert Witnesses

```
11. Identify each person whom you expect to call as an expert 
    witness at trial, stating: name, address, field of expertise, 
    and the subject matter of expected testimony.

12. For each expert identified, state the substance of the facts 
    and opinions to which the expert is expected to testify and 
    a summary of the grounds for each opinion.
```

### Prior Claims

```
13. State whether you have ever been involved in any other motor 
    vehicle accident, and if so, state the date, location, and 
    nature of each accident, and whether any claim was made.

14. State whether you have ever filed any claim for personal 
    injuries or property damage, and if so, identify: date, 
    nature of claim, parties involved, and outcome.
```

### Contentions

```
15. State each fact upon which you rely to support your denial 
    of liability in this case.

16. Identify all affirmative defenses you intend to assert and 
    state all facts supporting each defense.
```

---

## Motor Vehicle Specific

```
17. Describe the vehicle you were operating, including: year, 
    make, model, VIN, and registered owner.

18. State whether any mechanical defect or malfunction of your 
    vehicle contributed to the accident.

19. State whether you were using any electronic device, including 
    a cell phone, at the time of the accident.
```

---

## Instructions to Include

```
INSTRUCTIONS

1. These interrogatories are to be answered separately and fully, 
   in writing, under oath.

2. Your answers must be served upon counsel for Plaintiff within 
   thirty (30) days.

3. These interrogatories are continuing in nature. If you obtain 
   additional or different information before trial, you are 
   required to supplement your answers.

4. If you object to any interrogatory, state the grounds for 
   objection with specificity, and answer to the extent not 
   objectionable.
```

---

## Definitions to Include

```
DEFINITIONS

1. "Document" means any writing of any kind, including electronic 
   data, as defined in CR 34.

2. "You" or "your" refers to the party to whom these interrogatories 
   are directed, including agents, employees, and attorneys.

3. "Identify" when used with respect to a person means to state 
   the person's full name, current address, telephone number, and 
   relationship to you.

4. "Identify" when used with respect to a document means to state 
   the date, author, recipient, subject matter, and current location 
   or custodian of the document.
```
