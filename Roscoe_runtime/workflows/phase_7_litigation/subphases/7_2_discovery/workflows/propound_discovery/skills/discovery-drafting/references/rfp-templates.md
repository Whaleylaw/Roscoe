# Request for Production Templates Reference

## Standard PI Document Requests

### Insurance Documents

```
REQUEST NO. 1: All liability insurance policies providing coverage 
for the claims alleged in Plaintiff's Complaint.

REQUEST NO. 2: All declarations pages for any insurance policy 
identified in response to Request No. 1.

REQUEST NO. 3: All documents reflecting the policy limits of any 
insurance policy that may provide coverage for this claim.
```

### Accident-Related Documents

```
REQUEST NO. 4: All photographs, videos, or other visual recordings 
of the accident scene, the vehicles involved, or the injuries 
claimed in this lawsuit.

REQUEST NO. 5: All incident reports, accident reports, or other 
reports relating to the accident that is the subject of this lawsuit.

REQUEST NO. 6: All written or recorded statements made by any 
person regarding the accident.

REQUEST NO. 7: All communications with any person regarding the 
accident, including but not limited to text messages, emails, 
and social media posts.
```

### Vehicle Documents (MVA)

```
REQUEST NO. 8: All maintenance records for the vehicle you were 
operating at the time of the accident for the two years preceding 
the accident.

REQUEST NO. 9: All inspection records or repair invoices for the 
vehicle for the two years preceding the accident.

REQUEST NO. 10: The police report from this accident.
```

### Medical Documents

```
REQUEST NO. 11: All medical records, reports, or other documents 
relating to injuries allegedly caused by the accident.

REQUEST NO. 12: All independent medical examination reports 
obtained regarding Plaintiff.

REQUEST NO. 13: All documents reflecting medical treatment 
Plaintiff received following the accident.
```

### Expert Documents

```
REQUEST NO. 14: All reports prepared by any expert witness you 
intend to call at trial.

REQUEST NO. 15: All documents provided to or relied upon by any 
expert witness in forming their opinions.

REQUEST NO. 16: All curriculum vitae for any expert witness.
```

### Prior Claims Documents

```
REQUEST NO. 17: All documents relating to any other motor vehicle 
accident in which you have been involved in the past ten years.

REQUEST NO. 18: All documents relating to any personal injury 
claim you have made in the past ten years.
```

### Employment Documents (if relevant)

```
REQUEST NO. 19: Your personnel file for the driver involved in 
this accident.

REQUEST NO. 20: All driver qualification files for the driver, 
including CDL records, medical certifications, and training records.

REQUEST NO. 21: All drug and alcohol testing records for the driver.
```

## Premises Liability Specific

```
REQUEST NO. [X]: All inspection reports for the premises for the 
year preceding the accident.

REQUEST NO. [X]: All maintenance records for the area where the 
accident occurred.

REQUEST NO. [X]: All documents reflecting prior incidents at the 
same location.

REQUEST NO. [X]: All policies and procedures relating to 
maintenance, inspection, or safety at the premises.

REQUEST NO. [X]: All photographs of the area where the accident 
occurred taken within one year before or after the accident.
```

---

## Instructions to Include

```
INSTRUCTIONS

1. These requests are made pursuant to CR 34 of the Kentucky 
   Rules of Civil Procedure.

2. You shall produce the requested documents within thirty (30) 
   days of service.

3. Documents shall be produced as they are kept in the usual 
   course of business or shall be organized and labeled to 
   correspond to the categories in these requests.

4. If any document is withheld based on privilege, provide a 
   privilege log identifying: date, author, recipient, subject 
   matter, and privilege claimed.

5. "Document" includes electronic data, emails, text messages, 
   and social media posts.
```
