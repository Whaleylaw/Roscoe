# Deposition Defense Skill

## Overview

Train clients to give effective deposition testimony that is truthful, concise, and doesn't hurt their case.

## When to Use

Use when:
- Client's deposition has been noticed
- Need to prepare client for testimony
- Client anxious about deposition

DO NOT use if:
- Preparing to take defendant's deposition (use deposition-planning)
- Client already deposed in this case

## Workflow

### Step 1: Explain the Process

Cover:
- Who will be present
- Role of court reporter
- How questioning works
- Objections (still must answer most)

### Step 2: Teach Fundamental Rules

The key rules every witness must follow.

**See:** `references/preparation-checklist.md` for rule details.

### Step 3: Anticipate Topics

Standard areas defense will explore:
- Background and credibility
- Accident facts
- Injuries and treatment
- Prior conditions
- Daily activities

### Step 4: Identify Problem Areas

Red flags requiring extra preparation:
- Prior injuries
- Treatment gaps
- Social media posts
- Inconsistent statements

**See:** `references/common-traps.md` for trap questions.

### Step 5: Practice

Conduct mock examination on difficult topics.
