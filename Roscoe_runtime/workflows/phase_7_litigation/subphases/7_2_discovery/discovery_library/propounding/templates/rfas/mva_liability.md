## INSTRUCTIONS

1. If you cannot admit or deny a request in full, admit the part that is true and qualify or deny the remainder.

2. If you claim lack of knowledge or information, state that you have made reasonable inquiry and that information known or readily available is insufficient to enable you to admit or deny.

3. Failure to respond within 30 days results in the matters being deemed admitted per CR 36.01.

4. Per CR 36.02, if a party fails to admit and the requesting party later proves the matter true, the court shall award costs of proof unless there was reasonable ground to deny.
