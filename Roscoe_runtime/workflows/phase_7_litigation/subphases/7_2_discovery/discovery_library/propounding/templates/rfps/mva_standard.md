## PLAINTIFF'S FIRST SET OF REQUESTS FOR PRODUCTION OF DOCUMENTS TO DEFENDANT [defendant.name]

Comes the Plaintiff, [plaintiff.name], by counsel, and pursuant to CR 34, requests Defendant to produce the following documents within thirty (30) days from service hereof.

---

## INSTRUCTIONS

1. Produce all documents requested in their entirety, without redaction or alteration.

2. Produce documents as they are kept in the usual course of business OR organize and label them to correspond with categories in the request.

3. If you object to any request, state the specific grounds for objection. If objecting to part of a request, produce the non-objectionable portion.

4. If any document is withheld on grounds of privilege:
   - Identify the document
   - State the privilege claimed
   - Provide factual basis for the privilege

5. This request is continuing; supplement responses as additional documents become available.

---

## DEFINITIONS

1. **"Document"** - Any written, printed, typed, or graphic matter, including electronically stored information (ESI), emails, text messages, social media posts, photographs, videos, and recordings.

2. **"Collision"** - The motor vehicle collision on [accident.date] that is the subject of this litigation.

3. **"You/Your"** - Defendant [defendant.name], your representatives, agents, attorneys, insurance company, and anyone acting on your behalf.
