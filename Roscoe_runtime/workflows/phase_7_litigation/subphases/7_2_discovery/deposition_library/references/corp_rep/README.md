## Key Principles

### Corporation's Obligation

When a deposition notice under CR 30.02(6) is served:
1. Corporation **must** designate one or more persons to testify on its behalf
2. Designated person must testify on matters **known or reasonably available** to the organization
3. Testimony **binds the corporation**

### "Reasonable Particularity" Standard

Topics must be specific enough that:
1. Corporation can identify the **scope** of inquiry
2. Corporation can **select** an appropriate witness
3. Corporation can adequately **prepare** the witness
