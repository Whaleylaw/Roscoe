# Sample CR 30.02(6) Topics by Case Type

## Overview

This reference provides sample deposition topics organized by case type. Adapt these templates with case-specific details.

---

## Motor Vehicle Accident - Commercial Vehicle

### Driver's Employer (Trucking Company)

**Employment and Qualifications:**
1. "The employment relationship between [Driver] and [Company], including hire date, position, and job duties."
2. "[Company's] hiring practices and procedures applicable to [Driver], including background checks, reference verification, and driving record review."
3. "[Driver's] driving record as known to [Company], including any accidents, violations, or incidents."
4. "The training provided to [Driver] regarding safe driving practices."

**Supervision and Policies:**
5. "[Company's] supervision of [Driver], including supervisory structure and monitoring procedures."
6. "[Company's] policies regarding hours of service compliance."
7. "[Company's] drug and alcohol testing policies and [Driver's] testing history."
8. "Any disciplinary actions taken against [Driver] prior to [date]."

**Incident-Related:**
9. "All facts known to [Company] concerning the collision on [date]."
10. "[Company's] investigation of the collision, including findings and conclusions."
11. "Any post-accident drug or alcohol testing of [Driver]."

## Premises Liability - Slip and Fall

### Property Owner/Operator

**Condition and Notice:**
1. "[Defendant's] knowledge of the [hazard/condition] at [location] prior to [date], including when and how such knowledge was obtained."
2. "All complaints, reports, or notifications regarding [hazard/condition] at [location] during the [X] years prior to [date]."
3. "All prior incidents involving [similar hazard] at [location] during the [X] years prior to [date]."

**Inspection and Maintenance:**
4. "[Defendant's] policies and procedures for inspecting [area] at [location] in effect on [date]."
5. "The inspection records for [area] at [location] for the [period] prior to [date]."
6. "[Defendant's] policies and procedures for maintaining [area/condition] at [location]."
7. "All work orders, maintenance requests, or repairs related to [area/condition] at [location] from [date] to [date]."

**Incident Investigation:**
8. "All facts known to [Defendant] concerning the incident involving [Plaintiff] on [date]."
9. "[Defendant's] investigation of the incident, including any reports, photographs, or video recordings."
10. "The identity of all employees on duty at [location] at the time of the incident."

---

## Insurance - PIP Claim

### PIP Insurance Carrier

**Coverage:**
1. "The terms, conditions, and coverage limits of the PIP policy issued to [Policyholder], Policy No. [X], including any amendments or endorsements."
2. "The effective dates of the policy and whether coverage was in effect on [date]."
3. "Any coverage decisions made regarding [Plaintiff's] claim, including the basis for any denials or limitations."

**Claims Handling:**
4. "[Carrier's] procedures for processing and evaluating PIP claims, including internal guidelines and timelines."
5. "The identity of all persons who handled [Plaintiff's] claim and their roles."
6. "All documents reviewed in evaluating [Plaintiff's] claim."
7. "All communications with [Plaintiff] or [Plaintiff's] representatives regarding the claim."

**Payments and Denials:**
8. "All PIP benefits paid regarding [Plaintiff's] claim, including dates and amounts."
9. "All PIP benefits denied, disputed, or reduced, including the specific reasons."
10. "Any utilization review, peer review, or IME conducted in connection with [Plaintiff's] claim."

---

## Insurance - UIM Claim

### UIM Insurance Carrier

**Coverage and Policy:**
1. "The terms and conditions of the UIM coverage under Policy No. [X], including coverage limits and any exclusions."
2. "The process by which UIM coverage limits were selected, including any rejection or selection forms."

**Claims Handling:**
3. "[Carrier's] procedures for processing and valuing UIM claims, including any software or guidelines used."
4. "All evaluations or valuations of [Plaintiff's] claim, including factors considered."
5. "The investigation conducted into the underlying accident and [Plaintiff's] damages."

**Bad Faith Elements:**
6. "[Carrier's] compliance with KRS 304.12-230 (Unfair Claims Settlement Practices Act) in handling this claim."
7. "The settlement authority established for [Plaintiff's] claim and any changes to that authority."
8. "The reserves set for [Plaintiff's] claim and any adjustments."
9. "Any surveillance or social media investigation conducted regarding [Plaintiff]."

## ESI Topics (Any Case Type)

### Email Systems
1. "All email systems used by [Defendant] during [time period], including software, versions, and hosting."

### Other Electronic Communications
2. "All electronic communication systems besides email used by [Defendant] during [time period], including Microsoft Teams, Slack, or similar platforms."

### Retention and Backup
3. "Backup and archive systems for electronic communications at [Defendant] during [time period]."
4. "[Defendant's] retention and destruction policies for electronic data."

### Litigation Hold
5. "The litigation hold implemented in connection with this lawsuit, including date implemented, scope, and notifications."

### Search Methodology
6. "[Defendant's] method of searching for ESI responsive to Plaintiff's discovery requests, including search terms, custodians searched, and date ranges."

---

## Nursing Home Negligence

### Nursing Home Facility

**Staffing and Supervision:**
1. "[Facility's] staffing levels for [unit/floor] on [date], including RNs, LPNs, and CNAs."
2. "[Facility's] policies regarding minimum staffing requirements."
3. "The supervision provided to CNAs regarding [resident care function]."

**Care Planning:**
4. "[Resident's] care plan in effect on [date], including fall risk assessment and interventions."
5. "The process by which [Resident's] care plan was developed and updated."
6. "Any changes to [Resident's] care plan following the incident."

**Incident and Prior History:**
7. "All facts known to [Facility] concerning the incident on [date]."
8. "All prior falls or incidents involving [Resident] at [Facility]."
9. "Any complaints or deficiencies cited by regulatory agencies during [time period]."

---

## Medical Malpractice

### Hospital/Healthcare Facility

**Policies and Protocols:**
1. "[Hospital's] policies and protocols for [procedure/treatment] in effect on [date]."
2. "The standard of care at [Hospital] for [condition/procedure] during [time period]."
3. "[Hospital's] credentialing of [Provider] to perform [procedure]."

**Incident Investigation:**
4. "All facts known to [Hospital] concerning [Plaintiff's] care and treatment."
5. "[Hospital's] peer review or quality assurance investigation of [incident]."
6. "Any root cause analysis conducted regarding [incident]."

**Documentation:**
7. "[Hospital's] policies for medical record documentation."
8. "Any amendments, additions, or corrections to [Plaintiff's] medical records."
