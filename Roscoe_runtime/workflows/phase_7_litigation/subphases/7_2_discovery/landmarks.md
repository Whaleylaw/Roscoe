# Sub-Phase 7.2: Discovery - Landmarks

## Landmark Summary

| ID | Landmark | Type | Workflow |
|----|----------|------|----------|
| 7.2.1 | Our Discovery Propounded | Progress | propound_discovery |
| 7.2.2 | Defendant Responses Received | Progress | review_responses |
| 7.2.3 | Our Responses Served | Progress | respond_to_discovery |
| 7.2.4 | Client Deposition Complete | Progress | client_deposition_prep |
| 7.2.5 | Defendant Deposition Complete | Progress | party_depositions |
| 7.2.6 | Discovery Cutoff Passed | Exit | - |

---

## Landmark Definitions

### 7.2.1: Our Discovery Propounded

**Verification:** Interrogatories, RFPs, and RFAs served on defendant.

### 7.2.2: Defendant Responses Received

**Verification:** Responses to our discovery received (may be deficient).

### 7.2.3: Our Responses Served

**Verification:** Our responses to defendant's discovery served timely.

### 7.2.4: Client Deposition Complete

**Verification:** Client's deposition taken and transcript ordered.

### 7.2.5: Defendant Deposition Complete

**Verification:** Defendant's deposition taken and transcript ordered.

### 7.2.6: Discovery Cutoff Passed

**Exit Criterion**

**Verification:** Discovery cutoff date from scheduling order has passed.

---

## Exit to 7.3 Mediation

**Required:** 7.2.6 (Discovery Cutoff) OR substantial completion of discovery
