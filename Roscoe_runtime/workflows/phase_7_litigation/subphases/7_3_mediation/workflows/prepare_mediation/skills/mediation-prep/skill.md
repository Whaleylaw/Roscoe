# Mediation Prep Skill

## Overview

Develop comprehensive mediation materials to support effective settlement negotiation.

## When to Use

Use when:
- Mediation date scheduled
- Need to prepare mediation brief
- Compiling damage summary for settlement

DO NOT use if:
- Preparing for trial (different materials)
- At mediation session (use mediation-strategy)

## Workflow

### Step 1: Assess Case Strength

Evaluate:
- Liability clarity
- Damages proof
- Comparative fault issues
- Coverage available

### Step 2: Draft Brief Sections

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| Case Summary | Facts and procedural history |
| Liability | Strengths and weaknesses |
| Damages | Medical, lost wages, pain/suffering |
| Settlement History | Prior offers and demands |
| Demand | Current position with justification |

**See:** `references/mediation-brief-template.md` for format.

### Step 3: Prepare Damage Presentation

Compile:
- Medical specials with breakdown
- Future medical estimates
- Lost wage calculation
- Pain and suffering analysis

**See:** `references/damage-summary.md` for format.

### Step 4: Client Preparation Outline

Topics to cover:
- What to expect
- Settlement authority discussion
- Role in session

## Output Format

```markdown
## Mediation Preparation Complete

**Mediation Date:** [Date]
**Mediator:** [Name]

### Materials Prepared
- [ ] Mediation brief
- [ ] Damage summary
- [ ] Settlement analysis
- [ ] Client authority memo

### Settlement Range
- Demand: $[X]
- Target: $[Y]
- Authority: $[Z]
```
