# Witness List Reference

## Standard Format

```
PLAINTIFF'S WITNESS LIST

[Case Caption]
Civil Action No. [Number]

| # | Witness | Address | Description of Testimony |
|---|---------|---------|-------------------------|
| 1 | [Name] | [Address] | [Summary] |
| 2 | [Name] | [Address] | [Summary] |

                    Respectfully submitted,
                    [Signature block]
```

---

## Common PI Witnesses

### Liability Witnesses

| Witness | Testimony |
|---------|-----------|
| Plaintiff | Accident facts, injuries, damages |
| Eyewitnesses | What they saw/heard |
| Police officer | Investigation, scene, statements |
| Defendant | Admissions, conduct, knowledge |

### Medical Witnesses

| Witness | Testimony |
|---------|-----------|
| Treating physician | Treatment, causation, prognosis |
| Surgeon | Procedures performed |
| Physical therapist | Treatment, progress |
| IME physician | Response to defense claims |

### Damage Witnesses

| Witness | Testimony |
|---------|-----------|
| Employer | Lost wages, work capacity |
| Family member | Impact on daily life |
| Economist | Future lost earnings |
| Life care planner | Future medical needs |

### Expert Witnesses

| Witness | Testimony |
|---------|-----------|
| Accident reconstructionist | How accident occurred |
| Medical expert | Injuries and causation |
| Economic expert | Damages calculation |

---

## Witness Order Strategy

### Traditional Order

1. **Plaintiff** - Tell the story
2. **Liability witnesses** - Establish fault
3. **Medical witnesses** - Show injuries
4. **Damage witnesses** - Prove losses
5. **Strong finish** - Compelling witness

### Primacy and Recency

- **First witness:** Sets tone, memorable
- **Last witness:** Final impression
- **Middle:** Technical, detailed witnesses

## Witness Contact Information

Maintain current:
- Phone numbers
- Addresses
- Email
- Availability dates
- Special needs (interpreter, etc.)

---

## Subpoenas

For non-party witnesses:
- Serve subpoena in advance
- Include witness fee
- Confirm attendance
- Have backup plan

For records (subpoena duces tecum):
- Specific documents listed
- Reasonable time to comply
- Consider authentication issues
