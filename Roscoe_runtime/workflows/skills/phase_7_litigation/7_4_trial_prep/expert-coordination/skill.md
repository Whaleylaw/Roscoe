# Expert Coordination Skill

## Overview

Manage expert witness disclosure requirements and preparation for testimony.

## When to Use

Use when:
- Expert disclosure deadline approaching
- Preparing expert for deposition
- Analyzing opposing expert disclosure

DO NOT use if:
- Drafting discovery (use discovery-drafting)
- Preparing lay witnesses

## Workflow

### Step 1: Identify Required Disclosures

Per scheduling order, determine:
- Disclosure deadline
- Required content
- Rebuttal expert deadline

### Step 2: Draft Disclosure

Required elements:
- Expert identity and qualifications
- Subject matter of testimony
- Opinions and bases
- Exhibits to be used
- Compensation
- Prior testimony

**See:** `references/disclosure-requirements.md` for format.

### Step 3: Track Deadlines

| Deadline | Date |

| Rebuttal disclosure | [Date] |
