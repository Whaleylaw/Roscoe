# Expert Disclosure Requirements Reference

## Kentucky Expert Disclosure (CR 26.02(4))

### Required Information

Expert disclosure must include:

1. **Identity**
   - Full name
   - Address
   - Qualifications (CV attached)

2. **Subject Matter**
   - Topics on which expert will testify

3. **Opinions**
   - Complete statement of opinions
   - Bases and reasons for each opinion

4. **Data/Information**
   - All data or information considered

5. **Exhibits**
   - Exhibits to be used to summarize or support

6. **Qualifications**
   - Publications in past 10 years

7. **Compensation**
   - Compensation for study and testimony

8. **Prior Testimony**
   - List of cases testified in past 4 years

---

## Disclosure Format

```
EXPERT WITNESS DISCLOSURE

Pursuant to CR 26.02(4), Plaintiff discloses the following 
expert witness:

I. IDENTIFICATION

Name: [Full name]
Address: [Business address]
Qualifications: See attached curriculum vitae (Exhibit A)

II. SUBJECT MATTER

[Expert] will testify regarding [general subject matter].

III. OPINIONS

Opinion 1: [State opinion]
Basis: [Explain basis]

Opinion 2: [State opinion]
Basis: [Explain basis]

IV. DATA AND INFORMATION CONSIDERED

[Expert] considered the following in forming opinions:
- [List materials reviewed]
- [Medical records]
- [Depositions]
- [Other materials]

V. EXHIBITS

The following exhibits may be used:
- [List exhibits]

VI. COMPENSATION

[Expert] charges $[X] per hour for case review and 
$[Y] per hour for testimony.

VII. PRIOR TESTIMONY

See attached list of cases (Exhibit B).

                    [Signature block]
```

## Common PI Experts

### Medical Experts

- Treating physicians (hybrid fact/expert)
- Independent medical examiners (defense)
- Specialists (orthopedic, neurology, etc.)
- Life care planners

### Liability Experts

- Accident reconstructionists
- Human factors experts
- Safety experts

### Economic Experts

- Economists (lost earnings)
- Vocational experts (employability)
- Life care planners (future medical)

---

## Disclosure Timing Strategy

| Expert Type | Strategy |
|-------------|----------|
| Treating physician | May not need formal disclosure |
| Retained expert | Full disclosure required |
| Rebuttal expert | Wait for their disclosure first |

---

## Supplementation Duty

Must supplement disclosures if:
- Expert changes opinion
- New information obtained
- Original disclosure incomplete

Supplement promptly - delayed supplementation may result in exclusion.
