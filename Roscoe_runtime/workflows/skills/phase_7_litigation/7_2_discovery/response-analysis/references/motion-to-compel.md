# Motion to Compel Reference

## Prerequisites

Before filing motion to compel:

1. **Discovery properly served** - Verify service was proper
2. **Response deadline passed** - 30 days or extended deadline
3. **Meet and confer completed** - Required by CR 37
4. **Good faith effort** - Documented attempt to resolve

---

## Kentucky Rule (CR 37)

Motion to compel is available when:
- Party fails to respond
- Response is evasive or incomplete
- Objection is improper

**Certification Required:**
Must certify good faith effort to confer with opposing counsel.

---

## Motion Components

### Caption and Title

```
PLAINTIFF'S MOTION TO COMPEL DISCOVERY RESPONSES
AND FOR SANCTIONS
```

### Introduction

Brief statement of what is sought and why court intervention is needed.

### Factual Background

- Date discovery served
- Response deadline
- What responses were received
- Meet and confer efforts

### Specific Deficiencies

For each disputed request:
1. Quote the request
2. Quote the response/objection
3. Explain why deficient
4. What response should be

### Legal Argument

- CR 26 scope of discovery
- CR 33/34/36 requirements
- Burden on objecting party

### Request for Sanctions

- Attorney fees for motion
- Expenses incurred
- Other appropriate sanctions

### Prayer for Relief

- Order compelling responses
- Award of fees and costs
- Other relief

## Sanctions Available

| Sanction | When Appropriate |
|----------|------------------|
| Attorney fees | Party's position not substantially justified |
| Strike pleadings | Willful failure |
| Default judgment | Extreme cases |
| Contempt | Violation of court order |

## Timeline

| Event | Time |
|-------|------|
| Responses due | Day 30 |
| Meet and confer | Day 35-45 |
| Motion filed | Day 45-60 |
| Response | 14 days |
| Reply | 7 days |
| Hearing | Per court |

---

## Opposition Arguments to Anticipate

| Defense Argument | Counter |
|------------------|---------|
| "We answered" | Show answer was incomplete |
| "Objection valid" | Explain why not |
| "Too burdensome" | Show proportionality |
| "Privileged" | Demand proper log |
| "Will supplement" | Not a valid response |

## Draft Motion Template

```
COMMONWEALTH OF KENTUCKY
[COUNTY] CIRCUIT COURT

[CASE CAPTION]

PLAINTIFF'S MOTION TO COMPEL

    Comes now Plaintiff, by counsel, and moves this Court for an 
order compelling Defendant to provide complete responses to 
Plaintiff's discovery requests. In support thereof, Plaintiff states:

INTRODUCTION

    1. On [DATE], Plaintiff served [DISCOVERY TYPE] on Defendant.

    2. On [DATE], Defendant served responses that are deficient 
       for the reasons set forth below.

    3. Despite good faith efforts to resolve these issues, the 
       parties remain at impasse.

DEFICIENCIES

    4. [Detail each deficiency]

LEGAL STANDARD

    5. [Cite CR 26, 33, 34, 36, 37 as applicable]

ARGUMENT

    6. [Explain why responses are deficient]

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff requests this Court:
    a. Compel Defendant to provide complete responses;
    b. Award Plaintiff reasonable attorney fees; and
    c. Grant such other relief as is just.

                    Respectfully submitted,

                    [SIGNATURE BLOCK]

CERTIFICATE OF GOOD FAITH CONFERENCE

    The undersigned certifies that counsel has in good faith 
conferred or attempted to confer with opposing counsel in an 
effort to secure the discovery without court action.

                    [SIGNATURE]
```
