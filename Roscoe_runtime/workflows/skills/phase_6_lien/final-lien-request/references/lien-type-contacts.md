# Lien Type Contacts Reference

## Overview

This reference provides contact information and submission methods for each lien type when requesting final amounts.

---

## Medicare

### Primary Contacts

| Entity | Purpose | Contact |
|--------|---------|---------|
| BCRC (Benefits Coordination & Recovery Center) | Report settlement, request final demand | 1-855-798-2627 |
| MSPRP (Medicare Secondary Payer Recovery Portal) | Online submission | msprp.cms.hhs.gov |
| MSPRC (Medicare Secondary Payer Recovery Contractor) | Written correspondence | See address below |

### MSPRC Mailing Address

```
Medicare Secondary Payer Recovery Contractor
PO Box 138899
Oklahoma City, OK 73113-8899
```

### What to Submit

- Settlement amount
- Settlement date
- Attorney name and fee percentage
- Case expenses
- Date of accident
- Medicare beneficiary information

### Timeline

- Final demand: 30-60 days after settlement info submitted
- Payment due: 60 days from final demand

### What to Submit

- Settlement amount and date

## ERISA Health Plans

### How to Identify Contact

1. Check Explanation of Benefits (EOB) for subrogation notice
2. Call member services number on insurance card
3. Request transfer to subrogation/recovery department

### Common Third-Party Administrators

| TPA | Subrogation Contact |
|-----|---------------------|
| Optum | 1-800-291-2634 |
| Rawlings | 1-800-424-3410 |
| The Rawlings Company | See plan docs |
| Conduent | Varies by plan |

### What to Submit

- Written request on letterhead
- Settlement amount and date
- Attorney fee percentage
- Request for plan documents
- Request for reduction consideration

### Timeline

- Response: 2-4 weeks
- May require follow-up

---

## Hospital Statutory Liens

### Kentucky Hospital Liens (KRS 205.715)

**Process:**
1. Verify lien properly filed with court clerk
2. Contact hospital's patient accounts or legal department
3. Request final balance

### What to Submit

- Settlement notification
- Request for final itemized statement
- Request for reduction (if applicable)

### Timeline

- Response: 1-2 weeks typically

---

## Provider Liens / Letters of Protection

### Process

1. Contact provider's billing department
2. Reference the LOP signed
3. Request final balance

### What to Submit

- Settlement notification
- Copy of LOP (if needed)
- Request for final statement

### Timeline

- Response: 1-2 weeks

---

```
This firm represents [Client Name] in connection with injuries

- Settlement Amount: $[amount]
- Settlement Date: [date]

- Expenses: $[amount]

amount to complete the settlement statement.

Please respond to:
[Contact Information]

Thank you for your prompt attention.

Sincerely,
[Signature]
```
