## PLAINTIFF'S NOTICE OF DEPOSITION OF CORPORATE REPRESENTATIVE OF {{insurance.company}} PURSUANT TO CR 30.02(6)

Please take notice that pursuant to Kentucky Rules of Civil Procedure, the Plaintiff, {{client.name}}, shall take the deposition of the **Corporate Representative(s)** of Defendant, **{{insurance.company}}**, with knowledge of the following subject matter(s). Defendant is required to designate one or more officers, directors, managing agents, or other persons to testify on its behalf regarding matters known or reasonably available to the organization, as provided by CR 30.02(6).

### Matters for Examination

The matters upon which examination is requested are as follows:

**Coverage and Policy:**

1. The terms, conditions, and coverage limits of the personal injury protection ("PIP") policy issued to [POLICYHOLDER NAME], Policy No. {{insurance.policyNumber}}, including any amendments or endorsements.

2. The effective dates of said policy and whether the policy was in full force and effect on {{incidentDate}}.

3. Any coverage decisions made with respect to Plaintiff's PIP claim, including the basis for any denials, reductions, or limitations of benefits.

**Claims Handling:**

4. The procedure by which {{insurance.company}} processes and evaluates PIP claims, including timelines for payment and any internal guidelines or manuals governing claims handling.

5. All facts known to {{insurance.company}} concerning Plaintiff's PIP claim, Claim No. {{insurance.claimNumber}}, including the identity of all adjusters, supervisors, or other personnel who handled the claim.

6. The documents reviewed in evaluating Plaintiff's PIP claim, including medical records, bills, and any other documentation submitted by or on behalf of Plaintiff.

**Payments and Denials:**

7. All PIP benefits paid to or on behalf of Plaintiff, including dates of payment, amounts, and providers paid.

8. All PIP benefits denied, reduced, or disputed, including the specific reasons for each denial, reduction, or dispute.

9. Any utilization review, peer review, or independent medical examination obtained or relied upon in processing Plaintiff's claim.

**Policy Interpretation:**

10. {{insurance.company}}'s interpretation and application of KRS 304.39-020 and related Kentucky Motor Vehicle Reparations Act provisions to Plaintiff's claim.

11. Any coordination of benefits issues identified with respect to Plaintiff's claim.
