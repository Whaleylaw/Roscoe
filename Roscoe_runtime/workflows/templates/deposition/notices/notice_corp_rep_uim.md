## PLAINTIFF'S NOTICE OF DEPOSITION OF CORPORATE REPRESENTATIVE OF {{insurance.company}} PURSUANT TO CR 30.02(6)

Please take notice that pursuant to Kentucky Rules of Civil Procedure, the Plaintiff, {{client.name}}, shall take the deposition of the **Corporate Representative(s)** of Defendant, **{{insurance.company}}**, with knowledge of the following subject matter(s). Defendant is required to designate one or more officers, directors, managing agents, or other persons to testify on its behalf regarding matters known or reasonably available to the organization, as provided by CR 30.02(6).

### Matters for Examination

The matters upon which examination is requested are as follows:

**Coverage and Policy:**

1. The terms, conditions, and coverage limits of the underinsured motorist ("UIM") coverage provided under the policy issued to {{client.name}}, Policy No. {{insurance.policyNumber}}, including any amendments, endorsements, or exclusions.

2. The effective dates of said policy and whether UIM coverage was in full force and effect on {{incidentDate}}.

3. The process by which UIM coverage limits were selected, including any documentation of offers of higher limits and any rejection or selection forms signed by the insured.

**Claims Handling:**

4. The procedure by which {{insurance.company}} processes, evaluates, and values UIM claims, including any internal guidelines, manuals, or software used in claims handling and valuation.

5. All facts known to {{insurance.company}} concerning Plaintiff's UIM claim, Claim No. {{insurance.claimNumber}}, including the identity of all adjusters, supervisors, claims managers, or other personnel who handled or reviewed the claim.

6. All evaluations, assessments, or valuations of Plaintiff's UIM claim, including the factors considered and conclusions reached.

**Investigation:**

7. The investigation conducted by {{insurance.company}} into the underlying accident and Plaintiff's damages, including all persons interviewed, documents reviewed, and any independent investigation conducted.

8. Any surveillance, social media investigation, or other investigation of Plaintiff conducted by or at the direction of {{insurance.company}}.

**Settlement Authority and Reserves:**

9. The settlement authority established for Plaintiff's claim and any changes to that authority over time.

10. The reserves set for Plaintiff's claim and any adjustments to those reserves.

**Bad Faith Considerations (if applicable):**

11. {{insurance.company}}'s compliance with the Kentucky Unfair Claims Settlement Practices Act (KRS 304.12-230) in handling Plaintiff's claim.

12. Any complaints, lawsuits, or regulatory actions against {{insurance.company}} relating to UIM claims handling practices in Kentucky within the past five years.

13. {{insurance.company}}'s practices regarding timely evaluation and response to UIM claims.
