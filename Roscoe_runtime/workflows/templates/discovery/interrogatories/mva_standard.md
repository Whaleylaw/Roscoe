## PLAINTIFF'S FIRST SET OF INTERROGATORIES TO DEFENDANT [defendant.name]

Comes the Plaintiff, [plaintiff.name], by counsel, and pursuant to CR 33 and CR 34, directs the following Interrogatories to be answered by the Defendant, [defendant.name], separately and fully in writing, under oath, with a copy of such answers served upon undersigned counsel within thirty (30) days from service hereof.

---

## INSTRUCTIONS

1. Answer each interrogatory separately and fully in writing under oath, unless objected to, in which event the reasons for objection must be stated in lieu of an answer and signed by the person making the objection.

2. An evasive or incomplete answer is deemed to be a failure to answer under CR 37.01(c).

3. If your answer is in any way qualified, please state the exact nature and extent of the qualification.

4. If you refuse to respond to any interrogatory on the basis of a claim of privilege or exemption from discovery, furnish a written statement identifying:
   - (a) The type of document (if applicable)
   - (b) The author(s) and recipient(s)
   - (c) The date(s)
   - (d) A brief description of the subject matter
   - (e) The basis upon which you claim privilege

5. You are under a continuing duty to supplement your responses per CR 26.05.

---

## DEFINITIONS

1. **"Document"** - Any written, printed, typed, or graphic matter in any form, including electronically stored information.

2. **"Identify" (person)** - Provide full name, address, telephone number, employer, and relationship to you.

3. **"Identify" (document)** - Provide date, author, recipients, description, and current location.

4. **"Collision"** - The motor vehicle collision on [accident.date] that is the subject of this litigation.

5. **"You/Your"** - Defendant [defendant.name], your representatives, attorneys, and anyone acting on your behalf.
