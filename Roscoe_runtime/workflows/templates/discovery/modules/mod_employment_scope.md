# Module: Scope of Employment

## Purpose
These questions establish the employment relationship and scope of employment for vicarious liability (respondeat superior) claims. Use when employer liability is at issue.

## Usage
1. Copy questions to base template
2. Renumber sequentially
3. Customize for specific employment relationship
