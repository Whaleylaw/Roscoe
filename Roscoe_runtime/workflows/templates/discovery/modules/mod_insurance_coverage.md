# Module: Insurance Coverage

## Purpose
These questions identify all applicable insurance coverage, policy limits, and any coverage disputes. Required early in litigation for settlement evaluation.

## Usage
1. Copy questions to base template
2. Renumber sequentially
3. Adjust party names as needed
