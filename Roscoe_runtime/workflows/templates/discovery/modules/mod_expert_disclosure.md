# Module: Expert Disclosure

## Purpose
These questions obtain comprehensive expert disclosure including identity, opinions, basis for opinions, and qualifications. Required under CR 26.02.

## Usage
1. Copy questions to base template
2. Renumber sequentially
3. Use for both initial disclosure and follow-up

---

## INTERROGATORIES - EXPERT DISCLOSURE

**INTERROGATORY NO. [X]:**
Pursuant to CR 26.02, identify each person you expect to call as an expert witness at trial. For each expert, state:
- (a) Full name and address;
- (b) Professional qualifications and background;
- (c) The subject matter on which the expert is expected to testify;
- (d) The substance of the facts and opinions to which the expert is expected to testify;
- (e) A summary of the grounds for each opinion.

**INTERROGATORY NO. [X]:**
For each expert identified, provide the following information regarding the expert's litigation experience:
- (a) A list of all cases in which the expert has testified at trial or by deposition within the past four years, including case name, court, and whether testimony was for plaintiff or defense;
- (b) The percentage of the expert's income derived from litigation-related work;
- (c) The percentage of cases reviewed for plaintiffs versus defendants.
