The only factual questions before the Court are: (1) whether the Defendants were negligent; (2) whether such negligence caused Plaintiffs' injuries; and (3) the value of Plaintiffs' injuries. Defendants' remorse or similar post-injury state of mind has *no* tendency to make the existence of any fact that is of consequence to the determination of such questions more probable or less probable.
