## SCOPE OF REPRESENTATION

Attorney agrees to represent Client in the above-described matter. This representation includes:

1. Investigation of the facts and circumstances of the incident
2. Negotiation with insurance companies and adverse parties
3. Preparation and filing of a lawsuit if necessary
4. Trial and/or appeal if necessary

This representation does NOT include:
- Criminal matters arising from the incident
- Appeals beyond the first appeal of right
- Collection efforts beyond initial judgment enforcement
- Any matters not directly related to the above-described incident

## COSTS AND EXPENSES

Client understands that costs and expenses are separate from attorney's fees. These may include:

- Court filing fees
- Service of process fees
- Deposition costs
- Expert witness fees
- Medical record retrieval costs
- Investigation costs
- Travel expenses
- Postage and copying

**Client authorizes Attorney to advance all necessary costs and expenses.** These costs will be deducted from any recovery. If there is no recovery, **Client is not responsible for repaying advanced costs** unless Client terminates representation without good cause.

## LIENS AND SUBROGATION

Client understands that medical providers, health insurers, Medicare, Medicaid, and others may have a right to reimbursement from any settlement or judgment. Attorney will work to identify and negotiate all liens, but Client is ultimately responsible for satisfying any valid liens.

## CLIENT'S RESPONSIBILITIES

Client agrees to:

1. Cooperate fully with Attorney
2. Provide truthful and complete information
3. Respond promptly to requests for information
4. Attend all scheduled depositions, hearings, and trial dates
5. Notify Attorney of any changes in contact information
6. Not discuss the case with opposing parties, their attorneys, or insurance adjusters without consulting Attorney first

## NO GUARANTEE OF OUTCOME

**Attorney makes no guarantee or promise about the outcome of this matter.** Recovery depends on many factors outside Attorney's control. Client understands that there is a risk of no recovery.

## TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time for any reason by providing written notice.

**If Client terminates without good cause:**
- Client remains responsible for costs advanced
- Attorney may be entitled to a fee based on the reasonable value of services performed

**If Attorney withdraws or is discharged for good cause:**
- Attorney will refund any unearned fees
- Attorney will provide Client's file to new counsel upon request

## DISPUTES
