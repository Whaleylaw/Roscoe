# Personal Injury Fee Agreement
*(For Representation for any bodily injury claim up to Trial in Civil Court)*

I hereby retain and employ the law office of The Whaley Law Firm, PSC (hereinafter "Law Office") as my attorneys to represent me in my claims arising from an auto accident on or about **{{ACCIDENT_DATE}}** in **{{ACCIDENT_COUNTY}}** county, in the state of **{{ACCIDENT_STATE}}**.

I hereby authorize any action on my behalf, including the institution of legal proceedings that The Whaley Law Firm, PSC deems proper and necessary.

I desire The Whaley Law Firm, PSC to provide legal representation on my behalf and I understand that the Law Office is willing to provide said representation for the consideration and according to the terms set forth:

## Attorney's Fee

> **I agree and understand that I will pay The Whaley Law Firm, PSC an attorney fee of 33.33% (thirty-three and one-third percent) of any and all amounts recovered from each and every Defendant prior to a lawsuit being filed.**
>
> **I agree and understand that I will pay The Whaley Law Firm, PSC an attorney fee of 40% (forty percent) after suit has been filed on any and all amounts recovered from each and every Defendant.**

## Costs and Expenses

In addition to the attorney fee described above, I hereby agree and understand that all costs incurred in pursuit of my claims shall be paid by The Whaley Law Firm, PSC and reimbursed from any amounts recovered. Said costs include, but may not be limited to:

- Court costs
- Subpoena costs
- Costs for photocopies and postage
- Depositions and court reporters
- Travel expenses
- Cost for obtaining medical records/bills
- Any other evidence independently organized
- Settlement proposal preparation
- Cost of expert witnesses
- Interest on case development loans incurred by The Whaley Law Firm, PSC for and on my behalf

**The attorney fee shall be calculated upon the full amount of any recovery prior to any deduction of expenses discussed herein.**

## No Recovery, No Fee

I also understand that **if there is no monetary recovery, The Whaley Law Firm, PSC will not seek reimbursement of any case expenses or other costs, or interest from me.** In other words, I understand that if I do not receive a monetary recovery, I will not be responsible for reimbursing The Whaley Law Firm, PSC for any expenses or costs that The Whaley Law Firm, PSC incurs on my behalf or on the behalf of my claims/cases.

## Medical Records Authorization

I hereby grant The Whaley Law Firm, PSC full permission to disclose my medical records and medical bills to any and all persons, businesses, insurance companies, or any other individuals or entities whom they deem helpful or useful to my claims/cases. This specifically includes any person The Whaley Law Firm, PSC may decide to have review, organize, or otherwise view my medical records and any person they may choose to help prepare a settlement package on my behalf or on behalf of my claims/cases.

## Client Cooperation

I further agree to cooperate and assist The Whaley Law Firm, PSC in the preparation of my claims/cases including attending any conferences, identifying and locating witnesses, and preparation of any materials requested by The Whaley Law Firm, PSC in a timely manner.

## No Guarantees

I further agree and understand **The Whaley Law Firm, PSC has made no promises or guarantees regarding the outcome of my claims/cases.** I understand The Whaley Law Firm, PSC will investigate my claims/cases and if after doing so, believe that the claims/cases do not appear to have merit or otherwise do not wish to proceed with my claims/cases, The Whaley Law Firm, PSC has the right to cancel this agreement and withdraw as legal representation of my claims/cases.

## Scope of Representation

I understand this contract **does not include any claims for 'loss of consortium'** for my spouse, my children, or any other person. I understand this contract does not include any services beyond the Trial of my claims/cases and that any and all appeals would have to be separately discussed and negotiated. The Whaley Law Firm, PSC is in no way obligated or responsible for the prosecution of any appeal under this contract.

## Communication Requirements

I further agree and understand that complete cooperation and regular communication is mandatory in the facilitation of my claims/cases and will accept any and all correspondence regardless of delivery method from The Whaley Law Firm, PSC as well as their agents and representatives directly related to my claims/cases.

I agree that if my contact information should change at any time during the processing of my claims/cases, I will immediately notify The Whaley Law Firm, PSC with my new contact information. **Failure to do so, and/or failure to respond to communication may result in The Whaley Law Firm, PSC withdrawing from my claims/cases without further actions.**

I grant permission to The Whaley Law Firm, PSC to assume I have received all correspondence sent to me at the contact information provided on the date of the agreement, even without acknowledgment of receipt, unless written notification has been given of any changes in my contact information.

## Entire Agreement

This contract represents the entire agreement between The Whaley Law Firm, PSC and the undersigned. Neither party is relying on any representation not specifically included herein. If the attorney, having made a reasonable investigation, determines that it is not feasible or proper for a claim to be pursued, the attorney may notify the undersigned of that determination and the attorney may withdraw from further representation of the client under this civil contract.
