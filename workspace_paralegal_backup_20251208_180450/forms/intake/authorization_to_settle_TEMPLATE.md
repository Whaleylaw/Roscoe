## ACKNOWLEDGMENTS

By signing below, I/We acknowledge and agree that:

1. **Voluntary Settlement.** I/We are accepting this settlement voluntarily after consultation with my/our attorney. I/We understand I/We have the right to reject this offer and proceed with litigation.

2. **Final Settlement.** I/We understand that this is a FINAL settlement. Once I/We sign the release, I/We cannot pursue any further claims against the settling parties arising from this incident.

3. **No Guarantee of Better Outcome.** I/We understand that if I/We reject this offer and proceed to trial, there is no guarantee of a better outcome. I/We could receive less, nothing, or even be responsible for costs.

4. **Settlement Statement Review.** I/We have reviewed the Settlement Statement above and understand:
   - The gross settlement amount
   - The attorney's fee calculation
   - All case expenses being deducted
   - All liens being paid from the settlement
   - The net amount I/We will receive

5. **Lien Satisfaction.** I/We understand that all valid liens must be paid from the settlement. Attorney has negotiated the liens listed above, but I/We remain ultimately responsible for any valid liens not listed.

6. **Tax Advice.** I/We have been advised to consult with a tax professional regarding any potential tax implications of this settlement. Attorney has not provided tax advice.

7. **Release.** I/We authorize Attorney to sign and execute a release on my/our behalf, and I/We will personally sign any release required by the settling parties.

8. **Disbursement.** I/We authorize Attorney to disburse the settlement funds as shown in the Settlement Statement above.
