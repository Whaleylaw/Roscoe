In accordance with **Kentucky Revised Statute 422.317**, please provide to us, in a sworn statement, any and all medical records along with the associated billing in your possession up to and including but not limited to the following within **thirty (30) days**:

1. Any and all office notes, medical history, physicals, consultation notes, discharge summaries, orders and progress notes

2. Any and all radiology, pathology, toxicology, oncology or any other laboratory records and results

3. Any and all nurse's notes, physician notes, emergency records, operative reports/summaries, in-patient notes

4. Any and all pharmacy orders, prescriptions and dispensing logs

5. Any and all health insurance information or policies of medical coverage

6. Any and all billing and payment records for each date of service
