## UM/UIM Reservation of Rights

As you know, under Kentucky law, under-insured/uninsured motorist coverage constitutes personal insurance and our client is entitled to make claims under these policies if certain contractual requirements are satisfied. Please allow this letter to serve as notice that **our client is reserving their right to pursue an under-insured/uninsured claim under any policy of insurance for which they qualify**.
