## Naming Pattern Decoded

### Pattern 1: Hospital - Department

**Format:** `[Hospital] - [Department]`

**Examples:**
- Norton Audubon Hospital - Emergency
- Norton Hospital - Neurodiagnostics
- Norton Brownsboro Hospital - Emergency

**Meaning:** Department within a hospital

### Pattern 2: Institute/Specialists - Specialty - Location

**Format:** `[Institute] - [Specialty] - [Location]`

**Examples:**
- Norton Surgical Specialists - ENT & Audiology - Madison
- Norton Neuroscience Institute - Neurology - Downtown
- Norton Heart & Vascular Institute - Vascular Surgery - Audubon

**Meaning:** Specialty clinic at specific location

### Pattern 3: Institute - Location

**Format:** `[Institute] - [Location]`

**Examples:**
- Norton Neuroscience Institute - West Louisville
- Norton Orthopedic Institute - Brownsboro
- Norton Cancer Institute - Audubon

**Meaning:** General institute location (multiple specialties)

### Pattern 4: Group/Associates - Location

**Format:** `[Group Name] - [Location]`

**Examples:**
- Norton Community Medical Associates - Preston
- Norton Community Medical Associates - Dixie

**Meaning:** Primary care clinic at specific location
