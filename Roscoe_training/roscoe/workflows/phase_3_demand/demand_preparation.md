# Workflow: Demand Preparation

## Phase: demand
## Goal: Draft, review, and send comprehensive demand package

---

## When to Trigger

- All records collected
- Medical chronology complete
- Damages compiled
- Client finished treating
- Attorney authorizes demand

---

## Inputs Required

- Complete medical chronology
- Damages summary
- All medical records and bills
- Expert reports (if any)
- Client's personal statement
- Photos/evidence

---

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Verify Readiness
Confirm all prerequisites:
- [ ] All medical records received
- [ ] All bills received and totaled
- [ ] Chronology finalized
- [ ] Liens identified
- [ ] Damages calculated
- [ ] Client finished treating (or MMI reached)

### Step 2: Gather Supporting Materials
Collect for demand package:
1. Medical chronology
2. Medical records (organized)
3. Medical bills summary
4. Photos (injuries, scene, vehicles)
5. Police/accident report
6. Wage loss documentation
7. Expert reports
8. Client statement

### Step 3: Draft Demand Letter
**Use skill: demand-letter-drafting**

Structure:
1. **Introduction**
   - Client identification
   - Date and nature of incident
   - Demand purpose

2. **Liability Summary**
   - Facts establishing fault
   - Applicable law
   - Defendant's negligence

3. **Injuries and Treatment**
   - Initial injuries
   - Treatment timeline
   - Current condition
   - Prognosis

4. **Damages**
   - Medical expenses (past)
   - Future medical expenses
   - Lost wages (past)
   - Loss of earning capacity
   - Pain and suffering
   - Loss of enjoyment of life

5. **Demand**
   - Specific dollar amount
   - Deadline for response
   - Settlement authority statement

### Step 4: Calculate Demand Amount
Consider:
- Total medical specials
- Wage loss
- Multiplier for pain and suffering
- Policy limits
- Jury verdict research
- Comparative fault issues

### Step 5: Prepare Demand Package
Organize package:
1. Demand letter (cover)
2. Medical chronology
3. Bills summary with itemization
4. Medical records (paginated)
5. Supporting documentation
6. Photos on CD/USB or cloud link

### Step 6: Attorney Review
Present to supervising attorney:
1. Summary of case
2. Demand amount rationale
3. Draft demand letter
4. Supporting documentation
5. Get approval or revisions

### Step 7: Finalize and Send
1. Make any required revisions
2. Final proofread
3. Send via:
   - Certified mail
   - Email (if accepted)
   - Fax (with mail follow-up)
4. Request read receipt / acknowledgment
5. Note deadline for response

### Step 8: Document Submission
1. Save final demand to case file
2. Update case status to "Demand Sent"
3. Calendar follow-up date
4. Note in correspondence log

---

## Skills Used

- **demand-letter-drafting**: Draft persuasive demand
- **damages-calculation**: Calculate total damages
- **settlement-analysis**: Determine appropriate demand amount
- **document-organization**: Organize demand package

## Completion Criteria

- [ ] All supporting materials gathered
- [ ] Demand letter drafted
- [ ] Demand amount calculated and justified
- [ ] Attorney reviewed and approved
- [ ] Demand package assembled
- [ ] Demand sent to insurance company
- [ ] Follow-up calendared

## Demand Amount Considerations

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Clear liability | Higher demand |
| Disputed liability | Lower/negotiable |
| Serious injuries | Higher multiplier |
| Soft tissue only | Lower multiplier |
| Strong causation | Higher demand |
| Pre-existing conditions | Consider reduction |
| Sympathetic client | Higher demand |
| Policy limits | May cap demand |
